Has the outcome of a traffic stop by race changed over the years?

You would expect that the arrests that result from a traffic stop should be the same across races - i.e., for 100 whites stopped and 100 blacks stopped, you would expect that the same percentage of those stops should result in an arrest. What we see here is that while those numbers have fluctuated over time, that there is still discrepancies across races.
